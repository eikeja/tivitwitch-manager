How to get Twitch Credentials

To use the VOD feature, you must register a free "App" with Twitch.

  1. Go to the Twitch Developer Console:
    https://dev.twitch.tv/console
  2. Log in with your Twitch account.
  3. On the "Applications" page, click "Register Your Application".
  4. Fill out the form:
    • Name: Give it any unique name (e.g., "MyTiviTwitchVODs").
    • OAuth Redirect URLs: This is required. Enter `http://localhost` (it won't be used, but must be set).
    • Category: Select "Application Integration" or "Chat Bot".
  5. Click "Create".
  6. On the next page, you will see your new app. Click "Manage".
  7. You will now see your "Client ID". Copy this value.
  8. Click the "New Secret" button to generate a "Client Secret". Copy this value immediately (it will not be shown again).
  9. Paste both values into the settings on this page and click "Save".
